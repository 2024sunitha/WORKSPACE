CAKE

Today we are going to show how to make Vanilla cup Cake

Beat the butter and sugar until sugar melts then add vanilla extract,eggs or curd (if eggless) and beat for 2 mins. Add whole wheatflour, backing soda, baking powder and salt and mix well with the liquid contents. Milk needs to be added to get the correct texture of the mix (not more than 1/4cup). Then add requried toppings like nuts/dry fruits/chocochips like and serve in cupcake pan. Preheat oven 350'c and place the pan for around 20 to 22 mins until baked. Use tooth pick to check if its backe
